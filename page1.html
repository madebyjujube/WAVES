WAVES is an event series that happens on a regular basis based on the lunar calendar and the moon's phases. Every new moons and full moons, a gathering takes place where you can allow yourself a moment to reflect on life cycles. Where you can accept and let go of worries that have been bothering you lately. Thank yourself in the present, and be ready for a revitalizing experience through sound.
